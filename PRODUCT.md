# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Muslims tracking missed (qadaa) prayers in their daily lives. The user opens this app multiple times a day to log completed makeup prayers, check their progress, and stay motivated to fulfill their religious obligation. Context is devotional and personal — a quiet moment of accountability between the user and their practice.

## Product Purpose

Help Muslims track, manage, and make up missed prayers with dignity and ease. Records which prayers were made up each day, provides clear progress feedback, and integrates with real prayer times. Success means the user feels supported in their spiritual practice, not overwhelmed by it.

## Brand Personality

Warm, traditional, trustworthy. The tone is respectful and encouraging — like a thoughtful companion that helps without judgment. The aesthetic draws from Islamic design traditions (rich greens, geometric patterns, calligraphic sensibility) rendered through a clean modern interface.

## Design Principles

1. **Devotional clarity** — The primary task (logging a prayer) must be frictionless. Every tap removes a barrier between intention and action.
2. **Respectful craft** — This is a spiritual tool, not a game. Every animation, color, and sound should reinforce reverence, not gamification.
3. **Progress without pressure** — Show streaks and stats to encourage, not to shame. The data serves the user's spiritual goal.
4. **Rooted in tradition, built for today** — Honor Islamic design heritage (geometry, green, calligraphic structure) through a modern, clean UI.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Sufficient contrast ratios, minimum touch targets (48x48), readable body text at default sizes. Designed for users who may pray at any age — text should be comfortable without zooming.
